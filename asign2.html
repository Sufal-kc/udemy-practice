Sufal's Recipe for the Aalo Paratha

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • vegetable oil
  • some soup seed

For the filling:

  • 1 cup grated potatoes
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions
  • corainder fresh
  • onion
  • garlic
  • required spices

  1. Take a bowl and add whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, salt,
  2. prepare dough and add the ingredians for dough
  3. put the filling and roll as preparing a normal roti
  4. cook it on a tawa
